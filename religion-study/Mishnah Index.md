# 📜 Mishnah Index

> *Reference index of the 6 Orders (*Sedarim*) and 63 Tractates (*Masekhtot*) of the Mishnah — the foundational text of Rabbinic Judaism, redacted c. 200 CE by Rabbi Yehudah ha-Nasi.*

The Mishnah (מִשְׁנָה, "repetition/study") is the first major written work of rabbinic Judaism, codifying the Oral Torah (*Torah she-be'al Peh*). It forms the basis of the Talmud (both Babylonian and Jerusalem) and remains the central text of halakhic study.
