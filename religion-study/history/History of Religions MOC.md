# 🏛️ History of Religions — Map of Content

> The historical section traces the evolution of religion from prehistory to modernity. This MOC connects the chronological and thematic notes below.

---

## Chronological Arc

| Period | Note | Coverage |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Prehistory to 200 BCE** | [[Origins and the Axial Age]] | Paleolithic → Neolithic → River-valley civilisations → Axial Age (India, China, Israel, Iran, Greece) |
| **200 BCE to 1500 CE** | [[Classical and Medieval Religious History]] | Hellenistic world → rise of Christianity → Rabbinic Judaism → rise of Islam → Buddhist expansion → Medieval Christendom → Islamic Golden Age → Bhakti movement → East Asian synthesis |
| **1500–1800 CE** | [[The Reformation and Early Modern Religion]] | Protestant Reformation → Catholic Counter‑Reformation → colonial encounters → early modernity |
| **1800 CE–present** | [[Spread and Transformation of World Religions]] | Mechanisms of spread, schism, encounter with modernity, secularisation, new religious movements, globalisation |
