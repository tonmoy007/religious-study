# 🏛️ Mesopotamian Stories — Map of Content

A structured catalog of narrative cycles in the Mesopotamian cuneiform tradition. Unlike the single-canon traditions (Bible, Quran, Tanakh), Mesopotamian stories exist in multiple recensions across two millennia — Sumerian precursors, Old Babylonian versions, Standard Babylonian editions, and Neo-Assyrian copies — often with significant variants.
