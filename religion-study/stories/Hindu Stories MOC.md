# 🕉️ Hindu Stories — Map of Content

A curated collection of story notes from the Hindu tradition — spanning the **Itihasas** (Ramayana and Mahabharata), the **Puranas**, and **Vedic narratives**.

---

## Itihasa — The Epics

### The Ramayana of Valmiki
The story of **Rama**, prince of Ayodhya — an avatar of Vishnu — whose exile, his wife Sita's abduction, and his war to reclaim her is the foundational epic of Hindu dharma.

| # | Story | Section | Key Figure |
|---|-------|---------|------------|
| 1 | [[Rama's Birth and Youth]] | Bala Kanda | Rama, Vishwamitra, Sita |
| 2 | [[Rama's Exile]] | Ayodhya Kanda | Kaikeyi, Bharata |
| 3 | [[The Abduction of Sita]] | Aranya Kanda | Ravana, Jatayu, Sugriva |
| 4 | [[Hanuman's Search for Sita]] | Kishkindha + Sundara Kanda | Hanuman, Vali |
| 5 | [[The Great War]] | Yuddha Kanda | Rama, Ravana |
| 6 | [[Sita's Trial and Return]] | Uttara Kanda | Sita, Lava, Kusha |

### The Mahabharata of Vyasa
The epic of the Kuru dynasty — a civil war between the **Pandavas** and **Kauravas**, containing the Bhagavad Gita, the most sacred Hindu text after the Vedas.

| # | Story | Section | Key Figure |
|---|-------|---------|------------|
| 7 | [[The Birth of the Kuru Princes]] | Adi Parva | Shantanu, Bhishma, Pandu |
| 8 | [[Draupadi's Swayamvara and Wedding]] | Adi/Sabha Parva | Arjuna, Draupadi |
| 9 | [[The Dice Game and Disrobing of Draupadi]] | Sabha Parva | Shakuni, Duryodhana |
| 10 | [[The Pandavas in Exile]] | Vana Parva | Arjuna, Durvasa |
| 11 | [[The Bhagavad Gita]] | Bhishma Parva | Krishna, Arjuna |
| 12 | [[The Kurukshetra War and Aftermath]] | Bhishma–Svargarohana | All major figures |
