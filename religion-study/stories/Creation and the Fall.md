# Creation and the Fall

> The first story in the Bible — and the foundational narrative for the Jewish and Christian understanding of the world, humanity, sin, and redemption.

---

## The Story

### Creation (Genesis 1–2)

In the beginning, God creates the heavens and the earth. **Six days of creation:**
- **Day 1:** Light — separated from darkness (day and night)
- **Day 2:** The sky (firmament), separating waters above from waters below
- **Day 3:** Dry land, seas, and vegetation
- **Day 4:** Sun, moon, and stars — as signs for seasons, days, and years
- **Day 5:** Sea creatures and birds
- **Day 6:** Land animals — and finally **humankind**, male and female, made **in the image of God** (Genesis 1:27). God gives them dominion over the earth.
- **Day 7:** God rests — blessing and sanctifying the seventh day

**The Garden of Eden** (Genesis 2): A more detailed account of human creation. God forms Adam from the dust and breathes life into him. Plants the Garden of Eden with the **Tree of Life** and the **Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil**. God creates Eve from Adam's rib as a "helper fit for him."

> [!quote] Genesis 2:24
> "Therefore shall a man leave his father and his mother, and shall cleave unto his wife: and they shall be one flesh."

### The Fall (Genesis 3)

The serpent (identified in later tradition as Satan) tempts Eve: "Did God actually say, 'You shall not eat of any tree in the garden'?" (3:1). Eve eats the forbidden fruit and gives it to Adam.

**The consequences:**
- Their eyes are opened — they realize they are naked and hide from God
- God pronounces judgment:
  - **The serpent:** cursed to crawl on its belly, enmity between its seed and the woman's seed
  - **The woman:** pain in childbirth, subject to her husband
  - **The man:** the ground is cursed — labor and toil, "dust you are and to dust you shall return"
- God makes them garments of skin and **expels them from Eden** — barring the way to the Tree of Life

## Theological Significance

| Theme | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Creation ex nihilo** | God creates by word alone — no pre-existent matter, no struggle |
| **Imago Dei** | Humans are made in God's image — the basis for human dignity |
| **The Fall** | Not a fall from being but from relationship — sin as rebellion, not substance |
| **Original Sin** | Christian doctrine (esp. Augustine, Paul): Adam's sin corrupts all humanity |
| **Protoevangelium** | Genesis 3:15 — the first promise of redemption: the seed of the woman will crush the serpent's head (read as prophecy of Christ) |

## Comparison with Islamic Account

| Element | Bible | Qur'an |
|---------|-------|--------|
| **Creation of Adam** | From dust, God breathes life | From clay, God breathes spirit |
| **Creation of Eve** | From Adam's rib | Not detailed — from a single soul |
| **Location** | Garden of Eden | Paradise (al-Jannah) |
| **Tempter** | The serpent | Iblis (Satan) — who refuses to bow |
| **Who eats first** | Eve | Adam and Eve together |
| **Sin** | Rebellion / disobedience | A mistake (*zalama*) — no original sin |
| **Result** | Fallen nature inherited by all | Forgiveness upon repentance — no inherited sin |
| **After expulsion** | Thorns, toil, pain | Guidance sent — "Go down, all of you" |
