# Gilgamesh

## Summary

Gilgamesh is the semi-divine king of Uruk (Erech), two-thirds god and one-third human. Hero of the [[Epic of Gilgamesh]] — the finest surviving work of Mesopotamian literature. He begins as a tyrannical ruler, is humbled by friendship with Enkidu, undertakes great adventures (killing Humbaba, the Bull of Heaven), and after Enkidu's death embarks on a desperate quest for immortality. He ultimately fails but returns to Uruk wiser. In death he becomes "Judge of the Anunnaki" in the underworld.
