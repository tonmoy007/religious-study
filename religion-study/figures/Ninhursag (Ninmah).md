# Ninhursag (Ninmah)

## Summary

Ninhursag ("Lady of the Mountain"), also known as Ninmah ("Great Queen"), Nintu ("Lady of Birth"), and Aruru, is the great mother-goddess of Mesopotamia. She creates humanity alongside [[Enki (Ea) — God of Wisdom|Enki]] in [[Atrahasis]] and [[Enki and Ninmah]]. In the [[Epic of Gilgamesh]], Aruru creates [[Enkidu]] from clay. The mother-goddess is essential to creation theology: she forms humans from clay mixed with the blood of a slain god. She is the protectress of birth and fertility.
