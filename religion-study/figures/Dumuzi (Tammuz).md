# Dumuzi (Tammuz)

## Summary

Dumuzi (Tammuz in Akkadian) is the shepherd-god and lover of [[Inanna (Ishtar) — Goddess of Love and War|Inanna]], figuring in the Sacred Marriage rite. In [[Inanna's Descent to the Underworld]], after Inanna returns from the underworld, the demons seize Dumuzi as her replacement. He spends half the year in the underworld and half with Inanna — the cycle of the seasons. In the [[Epic of Gilgamesh]] Tablet VI, [[Inanna (Ishtar) — Goddess of Love and War|Ishtar]]'s former husband Tammuz is said to be mourned annually. The "Wailing for Tammuz" is mentioned in the Bible (Ezekiel 8:14).
