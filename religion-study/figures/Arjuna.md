# Arjuna

> The world's greatest archer — the son of Indra, the recipient of the Bhagavad Gita, and the hero whose skill with a bow decided the fate of the Kurukshetra war.

## Character

Arjuna is the **consummate warrior** — skilled, disciplined, and brave. But he is also introspective (he is the one who questions the war) and sensitive (he grieves for his son Abhimanyu).

His relationship with Krishna is the heart of the Gita — the warrior and the teacher, the seeker and the god.
