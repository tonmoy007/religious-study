# Ereshkigal

## Summary

Ereshkigal is the Queen of the Netherworld (Irkalla), the sister of [[Inanna (Ishtar) — Goddess of Love and War|Inanna/Ishtar]]. She rules the "Land of No Return" from her lapis lazuli palace. In [[Inanna's Descent to the Underworld]], she traps Inanna and, with the concurrence of the seven Anunnaki judges, hangs her on a hook. In the [[Epic of Gilgamesh]] Tablet VII, [[Enkidu]] dreams of her in his vision of the underworld. In Tablet XII, she reads the fates of the dead. She is the dark counterpart to Inanna's realms of life and love.
