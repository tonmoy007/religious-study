## The Great Ennead (The Nine of Heliopolis)

The foundational gods of Egyptian cosmology, created by [[Ra — Egyptian God|Atum-Ra]] from the primordial waters of Nun:

| Generation | God/Goddess | Domain | Role in the Book of the Dead |
|------------|-------------|--------|------------------------------|
| **1** | [[Ra — Egyptian God]] (Atum) | Sun, creation, supreme god | Lord of the Solar Barque; creator; judge of the gods |
| **2** | [[Shu — Egyptian God]] | Air, separation | Separates Nut and Geb; supports the sky |
| **2** | [[Tefnut — Egyptian Goddess]] | Moisture, dew | Mother of Nut and Geb; the cosmic moisture |
| **3** | [[Geb — Egyptian God]] | Earth | Father of Osiris, Isis, Set, Nephthys; heir of Ra |
| **3** | [[Nut — Egyptian Goddess]] | Sky, stars | Mother of the gods; swallows/gives birth to the sun |
| **4** | [[Osiris — Egyptian God]] | Underworld, death, resurrection | The judge; every deceased becomes "Osiris N." |
| **4** | [[Isis — Egyptian Goddess]] | Magic, motherhood, protection | Restores Osiris; protects Horus; great of magic |
| **4** | [[Set — Egyptian God]] | Chaos, storms, desert | Usurper of Osiris; defender of the Solar Barque |
| **4** | [[Nephthys — Egyptian Goddess]] | Mourning, protection, the tomb | Mourns with Isis; protects the dead |

## How the Elements Connect

The soul's journey through the Book of the Dead involves all these figures in sequence:

```
1.  The soul dies → ANUBIS embalms, OPENS THE MOUTH
2.  The soul enters the Tuat → ANUBIS guides, GATE GUARDIANS test
3.  The soul reaches the Hall → THOTH introduces, ASSESSORS question
4.  The Negative Confession → 42 declarations to the 42 ASSESSORS
5.  The Weighing of the Heart → ANUBIS weighs, MA'AT measures, THOTH records
6.  Verdict: justified → HORUS presents to OSIRIS
    Verdict: guilty → AMMUT devours
7.  The justified soul → sails with RA on the SOLAR BARQUE
8.  The soul enters the FIELD OF REEDS → HATHOR offers food, NUT offers water
9.  The soul chooses transformation → becomes a HAWK, LOTUS, STAR, or GOD
10. The cycle continues → soul joins RA's crew, fights APEPI, lives eternally
```
