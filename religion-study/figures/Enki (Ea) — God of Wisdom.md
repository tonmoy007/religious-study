# Enki (Ea) — God of Wisdom

## Summary

Enki (Sumerian) / Ea (Akkadian) is the god of fresh water, wisdom, crafts, magic, and creation. He is the patron of Eridu (E-abzu temple). In the [[Enuma Elish]], he kills Apsu and later proposes creating humanity. In [[Atrahasis]] and the [[Epic of Gilgamesh]] Tablet XI, he saves humanity by secretly warning the flood hero ([[Utnapishtim]]/Atrahasis) to build a ship. In [[Enki and Ninmah]], he creates humans from clay. In [[Enki and the World Order]], he organizes the cosmos. Called "the prefect of the Anunnaki," he is the cleverest god, often circumventing the harsh decrees of [[Enlil]]. His name means "Lord Earth."
