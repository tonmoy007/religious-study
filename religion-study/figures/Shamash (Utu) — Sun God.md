# Shamash (Utu) — Sun God

## Summary

Utu (Sumerian) / Shamash (Akkadian) is the sun-god and god of justice, truth, and divination. He is the son of Nanna (Sin). In the [[Epic of Gilgamesh]], he is the patron and protector of [[Gilgamesh]] and [[Enkidu]]. He provides the winds that subdue [[Humbaba]], rebukes Enkidu for cursing the harlot, and tries to dissuade Gilgamesh from seeking immortality. In the [[Enuma Elish]], his emergence marks the dawn. His main cult centers were Larsa and Sippar. The Code of Hammurabi claims to be received from Shamash.
