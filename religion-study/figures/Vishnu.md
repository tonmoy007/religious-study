# Vishnu

> The Preserver — the second god of the Hindu Trimurti, who descends as avatars (*avataras*) to restore dharma whenever it declines.

---

## Biography

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Role** | Preserver of the universe; maintains cosmic order (dharma) |
| **Consort** | Lakshmi (goddess of fortune) |
| **Vahana** | Garuda (the eagle-man) |
| **Color** | Blue |
| **Symbols** | Conch (shankha), discus (chakra), mace (gada), lotus (padma) |
| **Abode** | Vaikuntha — the celestial ocean of milk |

---

## The Ten Avatars (Dashavatara)

| Avatar | Form | Purpose |
|--------|------|---------|
| **Matsya** | Fish | Saves Manu from the flood |
| **Kurma** | Tortoise | Supports Mount Mandara during the churning |
| **Varaha** | Boar | Rescues the Earth from the cosmic ocean |
| **Narasimha** | Man-lion | Kills Hiranyakashipu |
| **Vamana** | Dwarf | Subdues King Bali |
| **Parashurama** | Warrior with an axe | Destroys the corrupt Kshatriya caste |
| **Rama** | Prince of Ayodhya | Kills Ravana |
| **Krishna** | Cowherd / Statesman | Teaches the Gita; participates in the Mahabharata war |
| **Buddha** | The enlightened one | Sometimes included — teaches compassion |
| **Kalki** | The future one | Yet to come — will end the Kali Yuga |

| Story | Role |

| **The Churning of the Ocean** | As Kurma, supports the mountain; as Mohini, tricks the Asuras |

| **Rama's Birth and Youth** | As Rama, defeats Ravana |
| **The Bhagavad Gita** | As Krishna, teaches Arjuna |

---

## Theology

Vishnu is the **All-Pervading** — the god who enters the world to save it. He is not distant: he descends. His avatars walk among humans. The *Bhagavata Purana* is devoted entirely to his deeds and those of his avatars.
