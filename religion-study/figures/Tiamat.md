# Tiamat

## Summary

Tiamat is the primordial salt-water dragon-goddess, the mother of the first gods in the [[Enuma Elish]]. Her name is cognate with *tehom* ("the deep") in Hebrew. With Apsu (fresh water), she gives birth to the first divine generations. After Apsu is killed by [[Enki (Ea) — God of Wisdom|Ea]], Tiamat creates an army of monsters led by [[Kingu]] to avenge him. She is slain by [[Marduk]] in the central combat of the Enuma Elish; her body is split to form the firmament and the earth.
