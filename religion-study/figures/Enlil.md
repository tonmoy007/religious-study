# Enlil

## Summary

Enlil ("Lord Wind") is the air/storm-god, the king of the gods in the Sumerian pantheon, and the chief deity of Nippur (Ekur temple). He is the son of [[Anu (An) — Sky God|An]] and Ki. In [[Atrahasis]], Enlil proposes the Flood to destroy humanity; in the [[Epic of Gilgamesh]] Tablet XI, he is angry that [[Utnapishtim]] survived, but eventually blesses him with immortality. In the [[Enuma Elish]], he is named among the great gods. He decrees the kingship of [[Gilgamesh]]. He is the active, executive power among the gods — the one who implements decisions.
