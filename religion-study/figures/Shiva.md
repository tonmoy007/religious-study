# Shiva

> The Destroyer — the third god of the Hindu Trimurti — the ascetic god who dwells on Mount Kailash, smeared with ash, his hair matted, the Ganga flowing from his head.

## Theology

Shiva is the god of **paradox**: the ascetic who is also the lover, the destroyer who is also the renewer, the meditator on the mountaintop who dances the universe into being. The **Shiva Linga** — an abstract form — represents the formless, transcendent reality.

The **Rudra** of the Vedas becomes the Shiva of the Puranas. The **Shaiva Siddhanta** tradition considers him the Supreme God.
