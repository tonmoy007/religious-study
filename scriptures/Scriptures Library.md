# 📜 Scriptures Library

Full, **public-domain** texts of the world's major scriptures, downloaded and
structured for study. Reading companion to the analytical notes in
[[Religion Study MOC]].

> [!info] About these texts
> Translations here are **public domain** or openly licensed (each file records
> its translation in frontmatter). They are historical/scholarly translations
> chosen for legal availability — not necessarily the version a community uses
> liturgically today. For devotional or authoritative use, consult an official edition.

---

## ✝️ Christianity
- **[[Bible (KJV) Index|Bible — King James Version]]** — all 66 books (Old + New Testament)
- **[[Apocrypha Index|Biblical Apocrypha / Deuterocanon]]** — 14 books (Tobit, Judith, Maccabees, Sirach, Wisdom…)

## ✡️ Judaism
- **[[Tanakh Index|Tanakh — Hebrew Bible]]** — 39 books, Jewish order (JPS 1917)
- **[[Mishnah Index|The Mishnah]]** — 63 tractates, 6 orders (William Davidson Edition)
- **[[judaism-talmud/Talmud Index|The Talmud]]** — 16 tractates, Mishnah + Gemara (Soncino trans.)

## ☪️ Islam
- **[[Quran Index|The Qur'an]]** — all 114 surahs (Pickthall)
- **[[Hadith Library|Hadith — The Nine Books]]** — ~41,000 hadith across 9 collections (8 fully populated in English; [[09 Sunan ad-Darimi|Sunan ad-Darimi]] has no complete English translation)

## 🕉️ Hinduism
- **[[dharmic/01 The Mahabharata (Adi Parva)|The Mahabharata (4 volumes)]]** — complete epic (Kisari Mohan Ganguli trans.)
- **[[dharmic/The Ramayana of Valmiki|The Ramayana of Valmiki]]** — full text (Ralph T. H. Griffith trans.)
- [[Bhagavad Gita (The Song Celestial)|Bhagavad Gita]] — trans. Edwin Arnold
- [[The Upanishads (Part I)|The Upanishads (Part I)]] — trans. Max Müller

## ☸️ Buddhism
- [[The Dhammapada]] — trans. Max Müller
- [[The Gospel of Buddha]] — compiled by Paul Carus

## 🪯 Sikhism
- **[[Guru Granth Sahib Index|Sri Guru Granth Sahib]]** — all 1,430 angs (GurbaniNow / Sant Singh Khalsa trans.)

## ☯️ Daoism & Confucianism
- [[Tao Te Ching]] — Laozi, trans. James Legge
- [[The Writings of Chuang Tzu]] — Zhuangzi, trans. Herbert Giles
- [[The Analects of Confucius]] — trans. James Legge

## 🕊️ Latter Day Saint movement
- [[The Book of Mormon]] — 1830 text

## 🏛️ Ancient & Mythological Religions
- **[[Mesopotamian Scriptures]]** — Sumerian, Akkadian & Babylonian texts (Anunnaki, Enuma Elish, Atrahasis, Epic of Gilgamesh)
- [[The Egyptian Book of the Dead]] — trans. E. A. Wallis Budge (study: [[The Egyptian Book of the Dead — Stories, Figures and Connections]])
- [[The Prose Edda (Younger Edda)|The Prose Edda]] — Norse; Snorri Sturluson

## How to Study These
- **Read alongside the analysis** in [[Sacred Texts Overview]] and [[Connections Between Religious Texts]].
- **Comparative project**: trace one figure (e.g. Abraham/Ibrahim) across [[Bible (KJV) Index|Bible]], [[Tanakh Index|Tanakh]], and [[Quran Index|Qur'an]].
- **Search across all texts** with Obsidian's global search — every verse is plain text.
- Every book file links back to its index; every index links back here.
